Frequently Asked Questions

What is CacaoBeans?

CacaoBeans is a verified investment platform that runs on the Binance Smart Chain Network (BEP20). It uses the concept of buying CacaoBeans to process chocolates which are then sold for a profit. It uses the blueprint hybrid of a farm yield and mining pool wherein investors are rewarded with higher ROI by limiting selling of their chocolates and encouraging habitual refining of the chocolates to produce higher profits that can go as high as 10%.

What is the use of CacaoBeans?

The CacaoBeans are the lifeline of the chocolate production plant. It follows that there should be a healthy amount of CacaoBeans available in the system to be able to produce sustainable volume of chocolates that can be sold for profit.

What does the BUY CACAO BEANS button accomplish?

The action is similar to your DEPOSIT BUTTON where the chosen amount of BNB in your wallet shall correspond to the market value of the CacaoBeans. The CacaoBeans is the main ingredient in the production of the chocolates which can be sold for profit.

What does the REFINE CHOCOLATES button accomplish?

The action is similar to compounding your investment or channeling back your profits into the investment platform. Refining chocolates allows your investment to grow and gain higher ROI when sold at a later time.

What does the SELL CHOCOLATES button accomplish?

The action is similar to withdrawing your dividends. Selling chocolates will immediately credit the available BNB amount into your associated wallet.

Is there a minimum amount needed to buy CacaoBeans?

Yes, you need at least 0.005 BNB plus the network fees needed to execute the transaction.

Is there a minimum amount needed to Refine Chocolates?

Yes, the available chocolates must be equal to at least 0.005 BNB to run the refine chocolates process.

How much is my daily profit or ROI?

You can earn as much as 10% daily from your investment. However this can vary depending on your refining techniques and frequency, time and date of the deposit, and the TVL (total value locked) variances. The average daily ROI is usually from 6 to 10%.

What happens if I SELL CHOCOLATES regularly?

Just like any product, your chocolates will plummet in value until you will not see any value when you sell them for a repeated, cumulative number of times.

Is there a specific way to maximize the profits from selling chocolates?

While there is no strict blueprint to follow to produce the most expensive chocolates, we recommend REFINING THE CHOCOLATES AT LEAST 5x before SELLING them.

Do Chocolatiers (investors) who came in first have an advantage over those who joined later?

No. The system is setup to provide equal opportunity to any Chocolatier that joins the program. The only advantage the system provides is based on your refining techniques. For Chocolatier 1 who did 6 consecutive refinements before selling the chocolates will get higher daily ROI % as compared to Chocolatier 2 who did 5 refinements while both will have significantly higher ROI, than Chocolatier 3 who never refined his chocolates and went straight to selling them.

Is there a referral system?

Chocolatiers who encourage direct investors will be credited with 20% of their downlines' deposits and re-investments (Refine).
